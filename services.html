Our Services

Category 1

Counselling service

Personalized one-on-one counseling sessions with a licensed therapist to address various mental health issues, such as anxiety, depression, stress, and trauma. Individual counseling provides a safe and confidential space for clients to explore their thoughts and feelings and work towards emotional well-being.

Group therapy

Group therapy sessions that bring together individuals facing similar mental health challenges. Led by a trained therapist, group therapy offers a supportive environment where participants can share their experiences, offer and receive feedback, and learn coping strategies from one another.

Category 2

Mindful Living sessions

Mindfulness-based practices and meditation techniques to cultivate present-moment awareness, reduce stress, and promote emotional regulation. Mindfulness meditation can enhance overall well-being and help individuals better cope with life's challenges.

Service 6

Specialized counseling services for individuals struggling with substance abuse or addiction issues. Substance abuse counseling provides tools and support to help clients achieve and maintain sobriety.

Category 3

Stress Management

Educational workshops focused on teaching practical techniques and coping strategies to manage stress and anxiety. These workshops often include relaxation exercises, time management skills, and effective problem-solving techniques.

Resources

Articles that cover various mental health topics, symptoms, disorders, and treatment options. These resources aim to educate users and increase awareness of mental health issues.
